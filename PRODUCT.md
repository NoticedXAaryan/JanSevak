# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Citizens looking to understand and access public services via AI, and Government Administrators managing reports and queries. The primary context of this Next.js site is to serve as the public face explaining the JanSeva product to both citizens and government officials.

## Product Purpose
JanSeva is an AI-powered public services platform for India. It allows citizens to navigate government schemes, report issues securely, and access healthcare/agricultural information instantly via WhatsApp and Telegram. The purpose of this specific web frontend is a landing page to explain the product clearly, showcasing its capabilities without exposing private admin details.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, Modern, Accessible

## Anti-references
Cluttered and bureaucratic (like typical older gov sites), Generic SaaS layouts, out-of-date and unresponsive portal designs.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over complexity:** Government services are confusing; the interface should be the opposite.
- **Show, don't tell:** Use live stats, clear visual effects (like ShaderGradient and liquid glass), and interactive elements to demonstrate the platform's power.
- **Expert confidence:** The design should feel authoritative yet modern, instilling trust that this is a state-of-the-art civic tool.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Must hit WCAG AA contrast minimums. Needs to be accessible and performant across devices, acknowledging that the end users include diverse Indian citizens. Reduced motion fallbacks required for any WebGL or shader animations.
